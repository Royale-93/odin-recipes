Blueberry Muffins

Description:

The blueberries and the sweet batter are fabulous together. Favorites of all who have tried them. Quick and easy, made with few ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 ¼ cups white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour, divided
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ cup buttermilk
  • 1 pint fresh blueberries - rinsed, drained and patted dry
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
