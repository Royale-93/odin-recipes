Strawberry Pie

Description:

Strawberry Pie is one of the easiest desserts to make, yet, it is very easy to make. you could also top it up with cream to add extra flavor to this good looking pie!

Ingredients:

  • 1 (9 inch) pie crust, baked
  • 1 quart fresh strawberries
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¾ cup water
  • ½ cup heavy whipping cream
